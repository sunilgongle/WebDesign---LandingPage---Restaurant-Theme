The pleasure of Mughal flavours on your plate.




Mughal’s Arcadia

On the bustling streets of Mosque Road
in Fazer town, Sait’s Restaurant welcomes you to a magnificent and cherishable experience in dining.

We offer you a vivid array of assorted selection of authentic and exotic Mughal and Afghan delicacies to delight the soul of food lover within you, in every discerning regalement.

Our first glance is a throwback to your history textbook; there are fairy lights, ornate patterns peeking through the entrance, and a high wall that veils the spaciousness within. The setting is recollective of a Mughlai courtyard, with mesh, curtains, lamps on trees and canopies with low seating within. The nearby mosque's call to worship and sound of kitchen-clanging from behind are quite the appetiser.
